Between Potential and Ideal

A thought experiment on the moment possibility begins to take form.

Here, existence is not read as a closed answer, but as a movement: from possibility, through witness, choice and responsibility, toward a more fitting form of life.

There is no claim here that science proves metaphysics, or that poetic language can replace precision. It offers a reading model: a language that separates core, witness and application without closing the question too quickly.

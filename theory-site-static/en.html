Between Potential and Ideal

A thought experiment on how possibility becomes form.

The project reads existence not as a closed answer, but as a movement: from possibility, through witness, choice and responsibility, toward a more fitting form of life.

It does not claim that science proves metaphysics, or that poetic language replaces precision. It offers a reading model that distinguishes core, witness and application.

The philosophical essay and tightened version: the central terms and conceptual movement.

2. Witness

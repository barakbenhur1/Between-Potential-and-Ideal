About the project

Between Potential and Ideal is an open philosophical-literary project: essay, appendices, stories, dialogues and images that try to give language to the movement between possibility, form and responsibility.

What it is

It is a thought experiment, not a doctrine. It offers a reading model meant to be criticized, corrected and expanded.

What it is not

It is not a scientific paper, not a metaphysical proof drawn from physics, and not a substitute for academic research or religious tradition.

This is an open thought experiment, not a closed doctrine.

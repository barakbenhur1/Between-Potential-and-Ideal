Method

The site does not present a closed doctrine, but a thought experiment testing movement between philosophy, literature, science, logic and application.

Language and reading

This page is in English. The language switch points to the parallel page when one exists; source documents are kept unchanged.

What the text does

It offers a reading language: potential, ideal, witness, optimum, boundary and responsibility.

What the text does not do

It does not claim that physics proves metaphysics, that AI experiences like humans, or that a literary image becomes a scientific fact.

Domain markers

Formal claims, metaphors and literary witness are read differently. That distinction is required for a fair reading.

This is an open thought experiment, not a closed doctrine.

Self-critique and open questions

For the theory not to become a belief in itself, it must leave room for questions that threaten it.

Weak points

  • Do the terms stay precise when they move across domains?
  • Does poetic language reveal something or hide a need for definition?
  • Is science-as-metaphor marked clearly enough?

Questions for readers

  • Which chapter is easiest to attack logically?
  • Which scientific use needs a source or caveat?
  • Where does the theory speak too strongly?

This is an open thought experiment, not a closed doctrine.
